Who is Ben Vance?

From St. Louis Missouri

Benjamin Vance is an IT Help Desk Representative for Compass Health Network. He is an Air Force Verteran who spent 5 years as an Aircrew Egress Technician where he was awarded the Air Force Achievement medal. In his free time Ben hangs out with his wife Marlee and they have two cats, Bandit and Bella. Benjamin's current goal is to complete the Full Stack Development Immersive program through San Diego Global Knowledge University and then get a job in software development.
